A hackathon is an event where people engage in rapid and collaborative engineering over a relatively short period of time such as 24 hours.

Hack solo or assemble your dream team! The team size is capped at four students. If you're searching for fellow hackers, feel free to reach out to those who have been accepted.

Attendance is free and the food is provided courtesy of our team!

If you're a high school, college or university student do not hesitate to apply!
